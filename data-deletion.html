Data Deletion

Learn how to request the deletion of your personal data from Stylon.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data:

1. Open the app and navigate to Profile

2. Scroll to "Account" section

3. Tap "Delete Account"

4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm

Alternatively, you can contact us directly via email to submit a deletion request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon request, we will permanently delete your account information, personal details, usage history, preferences, and any content you have created within the app.

Data We May Retain

We may retain certain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you, as well as data required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or enforcement of our agreements.

Deletion Timeline

⏱

Estimated Processing Time

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your verified request.
