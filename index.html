Stylon

A brief tagline about your app

Your AI-powered personal stylist is here! Snap your clothes, get daily outfit ideas, and see how they look on you with virtual try-on. 4 free credits every week!

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

✨

SNAP & ORGANIZE YOUR WARDROBE

Take a photo of any clothing item, and our AI instantly detects the category, color, pattern, material, and style. Build your digital closet in minutes with bulk upload — add up to 20 items at once.

✦

GET DAILY OUTFIT IDEAS

Stylon generates fresh outfit combinations from your actual clothes every day. Our smart algorithm learns your style preferences and avoids repeating the same looks. Favorite the outfits you love, dismiss the ones you don't.

✦

VIRTUAL TRY-ON

See exactly how an outfit looks on your body before you put it on. Upload a full-body photo once, and Stylon creates realistic try-on previews for any outfit combination — tops, bottoms, dresses, and outerwear.

✦

PERSONALIZED TO YOUR STYLE

ake a quick style quiz to tell Stylon your dress code, aesthetic preferences, favorite color palettes, and styling goals. Every outfit suggestion is tailored to you — whether you're minimalist, streetwear, classic, or smart casual.
