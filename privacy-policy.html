Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-02-13

Divyanshu J built Stylon as a commercial app. This page informs visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

Stylon ("we","our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Stylon mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address.

Photos: You may upload face photos, body photos, and clothing item photos. These are stored securely in our cloud storage and are only accessible to your account.

Wardrobe Data: Information about your clothing items (category, color, material, style) that you provide or that our AI detects from your photos.

Usage Data: Outfit preferences, favorites, dismissed outfit combinations, and scheduling preferences.

Purchase Information: Records of in-app credit purchases are managed by our payment processor, RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized outfit suggestions based on your wardrobe
  • Generate virtual try-on images using your body photo
  • Detect and categorize clothing items from your photos using AI
  • Manage your credit balance and process purchases
  • Send outfit notifications if you enable daily scheduling
  • Improve our services

3. AI Processing

We use third-party AI services to power key features:

OpenAI — for clothing detection and outfit generation. Your clothing photos are sent to OpenAI for analysis. OpenAI does not use this data to train their models per their API data usage policy.

FASHN — for virtual try-on image generation. Your body photo and garment photos are sent to FASHN to create try-on images.

These services process your data only to provide the requested functionality and do not retain your images beyond the processing period.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase, a secure cloud platform. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Row-level security policies ensure you can only access your own data
  • Photos are stored in a private storage bucket with per-user access controls
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are stored in a secure vault
  • Server-side rate limiting protects against abuse

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management and virtual currency
  • OpenAI — AI clothing detection and outfit generation
  • FASHN — virtual try-on image generation
  • Apple / Google — sign-in authentication (optional)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the app at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen
  • Modify your wardrobe items, photos, and preferences at any time

When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed, including your photos, wardrobe items, outfits, preferences, and credit history.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted from our servers, including photos stored in cloud storage.

8. Children’s Privacy

Stylon is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
